Common Causes:

  • Dust buildup
  • Broken or clogged fans
  • Old thermal paste

Steps to Fix:

  1. Power off and unplug the PC.
  2. Clean fans and vents with compressed air.
  3. Ensure all fans are spinning correctly.
  4. Replace thermal paste if old or dried.
  5. Use monitoring tools like HWMonitor to check temps.
